HISTORY



  • January 1950

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Sinpack Engineering was founded in the year of 1950. The company began its lengthy lifespan originally by manufacturing rice-processing machinery and constructing water pumps.

  • May 1980

    An Agency is Born

    In 1980, the company developed the first fully operational packaging machine and ever since the company strives to produce and provide elaborate high-tech packaging systems capable of meeting every packaging need and requirement for every customer.

  • May 1980 - April 2010

    Golden years of service

    Spanning three decades of service, Sinpack Engineering was established as a trustworthy leading company in the Greek market and became renowned for developing and designing expert vertical and horizontal packaging systems, weighing systems and industrial automations for a wide variety of products.

  • April 2010 - December 2019

    New fields of expertise

    After 2010, the company continued with the production of several other industrial packaging solutions with a greater focus on research and design, especially with ice transfer and ice packaging and managed to create even more flexible and reliable packaging systems.
