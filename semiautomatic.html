SEMI-AUTOMATIC PACKAGING

VERSATILE COST-EFFECTIVE SOLUTIONS

Sinpack Engineering, always trying to find flexible and cost-effective solutions, offers an alternative packaging series combining automatic and manual procedures. The final outcome is a high-end packaging product that meets every high packaging standard and it is specially customized for every packaging need.

Sinpack Engineering's production program for the semi-automatic series covers a specific customizable type of mechanical assembly that can be combined with either a weighing system or a volumetric cup system and is able to package a wide range of many granular types.

VF100 SEMI-AUTOMATIC ICE PACKAGING MACHINE

The vertical automatic packaging machine is able to package many different granular products in cooperation with a weighing system or a volumetric cups' system in a direct vertical line providing high-speed and high quality accurate packaging
